Malawi CDF for Children Dashboard

This dashboard helps planners explore child-focused CDF investments, compare district and constituency service gaps, and produce print-ready allocation reports with GIS-backed roads and waterways data.

Map Analytics: Ward-to-constituency geographic context with district filtering.
Equity Tool: Weighted allocation model (children, schools, HCFs, CBCCs).
Exports: Multi-scope print and Excel outputs for national, district, and constituency analysis.

Population definition: Child population metrics are used for hazard exposure and child-focused funding indicators.
